Privacy Policy – Your Information, Protected with Clarity

At CoolSculpting Davinci Clinic NZ, we place the highest priority on the privacy and security of our patients and website visitors. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information we collect, how it is used, protected, and your rights regarding your personal data. We are committed to full transparency and compliance with New Zealand's privacy laws, including the Privacy Act 2020, as well as relevant international standards such as the GDPR where applicable. Our goal is to ensure you have a safe, secure, and personalised experience while maintaining complete control over your information.

What data we collect and how it enhances your experience with us

We collect information that is necessary to provide and improve our medical and informational services. This includes:

  • Personal Identification Information: Such as your name, email address, date of birth, phone number, and physical address. This is required for appointment booking, verification, and communication regarding your treatments.
  • Technical Data: Including your IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system. This helps us optimise website performance, security, and ensure our content displays correctly on your device.
  • Usage Data: Details of your interactions with our website, such as pages visited, time spent, and actions taken. This information allows us to personalise content and improve the user experience.
  • Health Information: When you book a consultation, we may collect relevant medical history and treatment goals to ensure your safety and suitability for CoolSculpting procedures. This information is handled with the utmost confidentiality.

The essential role of cookies in personalising your CoolSculpting Davinci Clinic NZ experience

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to enhance functionality and personalise your visit. We use different types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for basic functions like secure login (if applicable) and session management. The website cannot function properly without them.
  • Analytics/Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site, allowing us to improve navigation and content layout. We use tools like Google Analytics with anonymised data where possible.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences (such as language or region) to provide a more personalised experience.
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant promotions and information about our services based on your interests, in accordance with advertising standards.

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings. For more details, please refer to our separate Cookie Policy.

How we use your data responsibly to improve our services

Your data is used strictly for legitimate business purposes and to enhance your experience with us. These purposes include:

  • To create and manage your patient account (if applicable), verify your identity, and schedule consultations and treatments.
  • To provide patient support and respond effectively to your enquiries.
  • To personalise website content, treatment recommendations, and information based on your interests.
  • To monitor and analyse website usage in order to improve performance, security, and develop new informational resources.
  • To comply with legal obligations, such as age verification and health record keeping.
  • To send important service updates, appointment reminders, and (with your consent) informational communications about relevant promotions or new services.

Strict protocols for data sharing with trusted partners

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only with trusted third-party service providers under strict contractual agreements that mandate confidentiality and security. These partners include:

  • Payment Processors: Secure financial institutions necessary for processing payments for treatments.
  • CoolSculpting Technology Providers: For equipment maintenance and warranty purposes, anonymised usage data may be shared.
  • Analytics Providers: Tools like Google Analytics to help us understand website traffic and user behaviour.
  • Regulatory Bodies: When legally required, we may share information with appropriate health authorities or law enforcement.

All data sharing is conducted in accordance with New Zealand's Privacy Act 2020, and we ensure any international transfers provide an adequate level of data protection.

Your rights to access, correct, or delete your data at any time

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have significant rights regarding your personal information. You can exercise these rights free of charge by contacting our Privacy Officer at privacy@coolsculptingdavinciclinic.co.nz. Your rights include:

  • Right of Access: To request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: To correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Deletion: To request the deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: To limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: To receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Right to Object: To object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.

We will respond to any such request within 20 working days, as required by New Zealand law.

Keeping you informed – timely updates to privacy policies

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, services, or legal requirements. Any significant changes will be communicated to you via email or through a prominent notice on our website before the change takes effect. We encourage you to review this page regularly to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

The "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page indicates the most recent revision. Your continued use of CoolSculpting Davinci Clinic NZ after any updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
