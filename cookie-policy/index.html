Cookie Policy – Designed with You in Mind

This Cookie Policy explains how CoolSculpting Davinci Clinic NZ (coolsculptingdavinciclinic.co.nz) uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing and information experience. We are committed to transparency and to giving you control over your data. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. Your privacy and trust are of the utmost importance to us.

Unlocking Better Experiences: How Cookies Work at CoolSculpting Davinci Clinic NZ

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you interact with our platform, and improve website performance. For example, cookies allow us to keep you logged in during your session (where applicable), remember your language and location settings, and ensure that information about our services displays correctly. Without cookies, many features of our website would not function smoothly, potentially impacting your ability to book consultations or access important treatment information.

Data We Collect and Our Strict Commitment to Responsible Use

The information collected via cookies is primarily technical and behavioural. This may include your IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, time spent on the site, and interaction with content and service information. We use this data exclusively to optimise our website, analyse trends, and personalise your experience – never to identify you personally without your consent. All data handling complies with New Zealand's Privacy Act 2020 and relevant international standards such as the GDPR where applicable.

Third-Party Services: Ensuring Safe and Transparent Tracking

We partner with trusted third parties who may place cookies on our website to provide services such as analytics, advertising, and appointment booking functionality. For example, Google Analytics helps us understand user behaviour, while our booking system uses cookies to manage consultation scheduling securely. These partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and are prohibited from using your data for purposes other than those we specify. We regularly review our third-party partnerships to ensure they align with our privacy standards and New Zealand regulations.

Take Control: How to Easily Customise Your Cookie Preferences

You have full control over your cookie settings. Upon your first visit, a cookie consent banner will appear where you can accept all, reject non-essential cookies, or customise your preferences. You can also manage cookies through your browser settings – most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality, such as the ability to book consultations online or access personalised content. We encourage you to adjust these settings based on your comfort level.

Always Up to Date: Updates to Our Policy and How to Contact Us

We may periodically update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our services. The most current version will always be available on this page with the effective date clearly stated. We encourage you to review it occasionally. If you have any questions or concerns regarding our use of cookies or your data privacy, please contact our Privacy Officer via the contact section of our website or email us directly at privacy@coolsculptingdavinciclinic.co.nz. We are here to ensure your experience with CoolSculpting Davinci Clinic NZ is both informative and secure.

By using coolsculptingdavinciclinic.co.nz (CoolSculpting Davinci Clinic NZ), you acknowledge and agree to our use of cookies as described in this policy. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data protection and transparency for all our visitors in New Zealand.
